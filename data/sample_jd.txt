# Job Description: Senior Backend Engineer — Platform Team

**Location:** Bengaluru (Hybrid)
**Level:** Senior / Staff
**Team:** Platform

---

## About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team in Bengaluru.
You will own and evolve the payment-orchestration service — a high-throughput,
reliability-critical system that processes millions of transactions daily.

---

## What You'll Do

- Design and implement distributed backend services in **Go** with **gRPC**
- Own reliability, observability, and on-call for the payment orchestration layer
- Drive migrations from legacy monolith patterns to service-mesh architecture
- Collaborate with product and data teams on new payment rail integrations
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to engineering standards

---

## Requirements

- **7+ years** of backend engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in **Go** (primary) or Python (secondary)
- Production experience with **gRPC**, Protobuf, and service mesh patterns
- Experience at a **fintech or payments company** strongly preferred
- Proven ownership of a high-availability distributed system
- Strong understanding of observability: tracing, metrics, alerting

---

## Nice to Have

- Experience with **PostgreSQL**, Redis, Kafka
- Prior **Staff or Tech Lead** experience
- Background in **payment orchestration**, ledgers, or settlement systems

---

## Compensation

- Competitive base + equity
- Bengaluru hybrid (2–3 days/week in office)
